all my past & present music projects are listed in the discog section below with direct links.
i am moving all solo activities to the sbs bandcamp as subscriber only. this isn’t a money thing — it’s a fiver for the whole year, which is the lowest bandcamp would let me set. it’s just a way to keep things away from the algorithmic circus of streaming and share music with people who are actually interested, instead of watching it disappear into the digital void. i’ll be adding new bits all the time, plus whatever oddities I manage to rescue from old hard drives.
